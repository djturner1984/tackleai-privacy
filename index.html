2. What the App Does

TackleAI helps users identify fishing lures. Users can enter the name of a fish or upload a photo of a fish, and the app will suggest five suitable fishing lures for catching that species.

The app is intended for use by the general public.

3. Information We Collect

TackleAI is designed to collect minimal information.

We do not require user accounts, and we do not collect personal identifiers such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

However, the app may process the following information:

Location Information

The app may access device location services to help provide more relevant fishing lure recommendations based on your region.

Location data is used only within the app to improve recommendations and is not used to identify individual users.

Photos and User Uploads

Users may upload photos of fish for identification. These images are processed by the app to generate lure recommendations.

4. Information We Do Not Collect

TackleAI does not collect or store:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Account credentials
  • Payment information
  • Contact lists
  • Marketing data

The app also does not track users across other apps or websites.

5. Data Storage

Uploaded images or temporary data used to generate recommendations may be processed using cloud infrastructure hosted in Australia via Amazon Web Services (AWS).

We aim to minimise the storage of user data and only retain information for as long as necessary to provide app functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

TackleAI does not currently use advertising networks, analytics services, or third-party tracking SDKs.

If this changes in future versions of the app, this Privacy Policy will be updated.
